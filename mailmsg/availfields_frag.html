Available substitute fields:

%CONFERENCE_NAME%: Conference name

%CONFERENCE_EMAIL%: Conference e-mail contact address

%SPEAKER%: Speaker name

%SPEAKER_BIO%: Speaker bio

%SUBMISSION_TITLE%: Title of submission

Other fields: %SHORT_SYNOPSIS%, %LONG_SYNOPSIS%, %SUBMISSION_TRACK%, %SUBMISSION_FORMAT%, %SUBMISSION_DURATION%.

Special URLs:

  • %EDIT_SPEAKER_URL%: CAUTION link allowing speaker to edit profile without logging in
  • %SUB_FEEDBACK_URL%: link to page giving analymous review comments
  • %ACK_ACCEPT_URL%: for accepted submissions, link to page where speakers can confirm or decline acceptance
